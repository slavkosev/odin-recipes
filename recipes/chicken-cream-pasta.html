Chicken and eggs dinner

Description

This recipe consists of creamy chicken and pasta.

Ingredients

  • 250g Raw chicken breast
  • 250ml Cooking cream
  • 100g Uncooked pasta
  • 4 cheeses fix

Steps

  1. Fry the chicken on the pan
  2. Boil the pasta for 10 minutes
  3. Add the 4 cheeses mix and the cream to the pan with the chicken
  4. Pour the creamy chicken into a pot with the cooked pasta

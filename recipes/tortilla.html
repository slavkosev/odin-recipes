Chicken Tortilla

Two high protein chicken tortillas.

Diced chicken breast is seasoned and then cooked on a pan while on the side we've chopped cucumbers and tomatoes. When the chicken is ready we place it on the tortilla, add the vegetables, add our low sugar BBQ sauce and we wrap the tortilla.

    Ingredients:
  • 2 tortilla wraps
  • 200g chicken breast
  • Half cucumber
  • Half tomato
  • low sugar BBQ sauce
    Steps:
  1. Dice the chicken breast and the vegetables into small pieces.
  2. Turn on the stove and put on a pan and add butter or oil.
  3. Place the chicken breast on the pan and add seasoning of choice.
  4. Wait for the chicken to cook while stirring it.
  5. Put the cooked chicken on the tortilla wrap and add the vegetables and BBQ sauce.
  6. Wrap the tortilla.

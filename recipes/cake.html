High protein cocoa cake.

Quick and easy recipe for a microwaved high protein cocoa cake.

    Ingredients:
  • 10g cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 pint baking powder
    Steps:
  1. Break the eggs in a bowl and beat them.
  2. Add the cocoa poweder.
  3. Add the protein powder and the baking powder.
  4. Stirr the bowl till it becomes runny.
  5. Microwave for 3 minutes.

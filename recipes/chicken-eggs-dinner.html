Chicken and eggs dinner

Description

This recipe consists of baked chicken thighs with fried eggs and slices of toast and cherry tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • 300g Raw chicken thigs
  • 4 Medium eggs
  • 4 Slices of bread
  • 1 Cup of cherry tomatoes
  1. Bake the chicken thighs in the oven
  2. Fry the eggs on a pan
  3. Slice the bread and the cherry tomatoes
  4. Plate everything together

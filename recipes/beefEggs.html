Beef and Eggs

Beef mince with scrambled eggs with a side of avocados.

Beef mince is cooked and then plated while eggs are scrambled on the same pan with the lefover beef fats. Avocado is sliced and plated as well.

    Ingredients:
  • 200g beef mince
  • 4 eggs
  • Half avocado
    Steps:
  1. Place the beef mince on a pan on a heated stove.
  2. Break four eggs in a bowl and beat them up.
  3. Take out the cooked beef in a plate and place the eggs on the pan.
  4. Wait for the eggs to get ready while stirring the pan.
  5. Add the eggs and diced avodaco to the plate and season by choice.

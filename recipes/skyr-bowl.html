Skyr bowl

Description

This recipe consists of Skyr, raspberris, chopped up protein bar and granola of choice.

Ingredients

  • 250g Skyr
  • 100g Raspberries
  • 1 Chocolate protein bar
  • 50g Granola of choice
  1. Put 250g of Skyr in a bowl
  2. Put the raspberries inside
  3. Chop the protein bar and put it inside
  4. Top up with the Granola
